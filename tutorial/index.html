A guide for how to use Buyandship
See how easy it is to shop online at your favorite retailers and ship internationally with Buyandship.
WATCH VIDEO
How to use ?
Get your overseas addresses
Register with us to get your very own overseas addresses. Use them as your delivery addresses when shopping from worldwide online stores.
Start Shopping Online!
Buy from your favorite online stores worldwide and send your purchases to your new overseas address.
Declare your parcel
As soon as you’ve received the Tracking Number and Shipping Courier details from the seller, you candeclare your parcel on our website or APP. Click here to add a new Shipment” at our membership centre
Consolidation & Payment
When your parcel is delivered at our Hong Kong warehouse, payment is easy! You have options to pay by Buyandship Points / Credit Card Payment. We also offer consolidation service by combining multiple orders into one box to save you on the international shipping fee
Your box is delivered to you
Your parcel will be at your doorstep as quickly as 3 business days. Our online tracking gives you status updates as your shipment speeds through our website and APP.

Repeat these steps as many times as you like. We love getting packages just as much as you do.
