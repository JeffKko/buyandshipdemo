About Us

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Asia-Pacific's leading e-commerce cross-border logistics company. Present in the minds of all users, we strive to provide the most consistent and efficient services for our customers.

Positioning

We want to meet all consumer demands with an efficient, high-quality service that saves time and cost for cross-border online shopping. Closely resembling a shopping partner, we enable border-free shopping, while also ensuring a low price for international transshipment

What Can We Provide

We provide a smooth, transparent and reliable shipment process, with only a few easy steps, whilst also focusing on high quality customer service. We provide professional support - before, during and after sales; to enable the most enjoyable online shopping experience.
